Value added owner, operator, and developer of multifamily apartment communities.




AmeriSouth's focus remains on delivering quality places to live in the affordable rent market - continually improving product quality while reducing costs through unique efficiency enchancements. We maintain properties in 6 different cities throughout Texas: Dallas, Ft. Worth, Austin, Texarkana, Irving, and Grand Prairie.





Over the years, the company has completed acquisition rehabilitation projects on over 40 apartment communities. In addition to the expertise in acquisition rehabilitation projects, the company specializes in the restoration of its own apartment communities.




With the main office in downtown Dallas, AmeriSouth retains over 100 employees and has always placed great importance on creating a long-term relationship with its team members. Almost all of AmeriSouth's corporate employees and most of the senior managers have been with the company for more than ten years.
